Thank you for choosing the new Kentlands Dental Care team for all your dental needs. After changes in management June 2012, we have been working to improve our multi-specialty practice. It has been designed to offer excellent patient care an enables you to complete a wide range of dental and Facial services in one location. Our team of dedicated professionals includes General Dentists treating all family members, as well as specialists in Orthodontics, Endodontics, and Oral Surgery. Our team strives to provide you with the most exceptional care available. Our full service facility offers appointments in the evenings and on Saturdays for your convenience. We also provide emergency (pain) dental treatment during regular office hours; and are willing to accommodate you immediately. After-hours emergencies will be handled on an individual basis.
